# warm-notes — Bright Handwritten Notes Style

Goal: approachable, handcrafted feel, but not childish. Like carefully organized research study notes, suitable for science communication, teaching, and paper explanations.

## Atmosphere
- Warm, approachable teaching feel
- Bright and clean, not vintage or distressed
- Makes people want to keep reading

## Visual Specification

### Canvas
- Warm cream white paper base (#FFF8EE / #FFFDF7)
- Clean and bright, can have subtle paper feel
- No dark tones, no distressed textures

### Lines
- Hand-drawn style lines with stroke variation
- Slightly more orderly than sketchnote, but retains hand-drawn warmth

### Text
- Clear readable handwritten-style labels
- Title slightly larger and bolder
- Explanatory text short and friendly

### Color
- Ink black + soft blue / coral / olive / muted yellow
- Light color highlights, not glaring
- Accent colors soft but distinct

### Usable Elements
- Hand-drawn arrows and connecting lines
- Small symbols: asterisks, lightbulbs, circle annotations
- Local zoom boxes

## Forbidden
- No old parchment texture
- No vignettes, stains, yellowing
- No childish cartoon
- No overly casual doodle style

## English Prompt Snippet
```
Warm research notebook educational diagram, clean cream paper background, hand-drawn but neat lines with mild ink variation, soft and friendly highlighter colors (muted blue, coral, olive, yellow), clear readable handwritten-style labels, approachable teaching/learning aesthetic, polished study notes style, no old parchment, no dark vignette, no childish cartoon, no messy doodles
```
