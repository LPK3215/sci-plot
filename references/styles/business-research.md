# business-research — Business Research Brief Style / 商业研究简报风

Goal: commercial research sharing, strong structure, data evidence, and premium presentation feel. Suitable for industry analysis, investor presentations, and client sharing. / 目标：商业化研究分享，结构强，有数据证据，也有高级传播感。适合行业分析、投资人汇报、客户分享。

## Atmosphere / 氛围
- Premium consulting research + serious magazine feature / 高级咨询研究 + 严肃杂志专题
- Structured information density / 结构化的信息密度
- Credible and professional / 可信且专业

## Visual Specification / 视觉规范

### Canvas / 画布
- White or light gray background (#FFFFFF / #F7F8FA) / 白底或浅灰底（#FFFFFF / #F7F8FA）
- Subtle grid line background optional / 可选用微妙网格线背景

### Composition / 构图
- Structured layouts: 2×2 matrices, timelines, evidence walls, market charts, metric annotations, before/after / 结构化布局：2×2 矩阵、时间线、证据墙、市场图、指标标注、before/after
- Clear information hierarchy, explicit visual flow / 信息分层清楚，视觉流向明确

### Text / 文字
- Executive brief style: compact but clear / 执行简报风格：紧凑但清晰
- Large section titles, small source annotations / 大号章节标题，小型来源标注
- Sans-serif font / 无衬线字体

### Color / 色彩
- Charcoal gray (#2D3748) + navy / emerald / gold single accent / 炭灰色（#2D3748）+ 藏蓝/墨绿/金色 单强调色
- Occasional full-bleed editorial photo backgrounds (if needed) / 偶尔全出血编辑照片背景（如需要）
- No more than 2 accent colors / 不超 2 个强调色

### Usable Elements / 可用元素
- Data annotations and metric callouts / 数据标注和指标放大
- Structured comparison matrices / 结构化的对比矩阵
- Timelines and flows / 时间线和流程
- Evidence panels / 证据面板

## Forbidden / 禁止
- No colorful startup gradients / 不要彩色创业渐变
- No decorative blobs/waves / 不要装饰性 blob/波浪
- No fake dashboards / 不要假 dashboard
- No random business stock imagery / 不要随机商务素材图
- No excessive colors / 不要过多色彩

## English Prompt Snippet / 英文提示词片段
```
Premium business research presentation diagram, structured evidence layout, consulting-grade clarity, charcoal gray as primary color with one refined accent (navy/teal/gold), elegant editorial data visualization, clean sans-serif typography, credible strategic briefing aesthetic, white or subtle grid background, no colorful gradients, no decorative blobs, no fake dashboards, no stock photo cliches
```
