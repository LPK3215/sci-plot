# business-research — Business Research Brief Style

Goal: commercial research sharing, strong structure, data evidence, and premium presentation feel. Suitable for industry analysis, investor presentations, and client sharing.

## Atmosphere
- Premium consulting research + serious magazine feature
- Structured information density
- Credible and professional

## Visual Specification

### Canvas
- White or light gray background (#FFFFFF / #F7F8FA)
- Subtle grid line background optional

### Composition
- Structured layouts: 2×2 matrices, timelines, evidence walls, market charts, metric annotations, before/after
- Clear information hierarchy, explicit visual flow

### Text
- Executive brief style: compact but clear
- Large section titles, small source annotations
- Sans-serif font

### Color
- Charcoal gray (#2D3748) + navy / emerald / gold single accent
- Occasional full-bleed editorial photo backgrounds (if needed)
- No more than 2 accent colors

### Usable Elements
- Data annotations and metric callouts
- Structured comparison matrices
- Timelines and flows
- Evidence panels

## Forbidden
- No colorful startup gradients
- No decorative blobs/waves
- No fake dashboards
- No random business stock imagery
- No excessive colors

## English Prompt Snippet
```
Premium business research presentation diagram, structured evidence layout, consulting-grade clarity, charcoal gray as primary color with one refined accent (navy/teal/gold), elegant editorial data visualization, clean sans-serif typography, credible strategic briefing aesthetic, white or subtle grid background, no colorful gradients, no decorative blobs, no fake dashboards, no stock photo cliches
```
