# sketchnote — Warm Research Notebook Style

Like a carefully organized research handout: bright, warm, refreshing, yet still professional.

## Atmosphere
- Warm, personal, thoughtful
- Fresh and bright, like lecture notes / research handouts, not vintage old paper
- Not cold machine-generated graphics
- Invites close inspection, like reading a carefully organized page of method notes

## Visual Specification

### Base Color
- Bright light cream or buttercream (ref #FFF8EA / #FAF4E6 / #FFFDF7)
- Clean paper surface, can have very subtle paper feel but no obvious texture
- No kraft paper, old parchment, vignettes, stains, sepia edges
- Overall impression: "warm and clean", not "old and vintage"

### Lines
- Black hand-drawn style lines with natural ink stroke weight variation
- Not perfect vector straight lines — slight irregularity allowed, but not sloppy
- Thick lines = core structure, thin lines = auxiliary connections

### Text
- Handwritten style (English: sketch/casual fonts, Chinese: clear but slightly handwritten feel)
- Labels must be neat, not too messy to affect readability
- Title font slightly larger and bolder than labels

### Emphasis
- Accent colors: deep blue #2C5F8A / coral red #D85C4A / olive green #5C6B4F / soft yellow #F3D36B
- Emphasis methods: hand-drawn dashed borders, light color fills, marker underlines, local zoom circles
- No large heavy color blocks — keep it light and clear

### Information Density
- Main mechanism diagram should occupy 75%-85% of the canvas
- When whitespace is excessive, prioritize: local zoom boxes, step mini-examples, dimension annotations, before/after comparisons
- One figure covers one main mechanism, but covers it thoroughly

### Usable Comprehension Anchors (restrained)
- Small arrows (hand-drawn triangular arrows)
- Small circle/dot annotations
- Simplified small icons: magnifying glass, sticky note, lightbulb, bracket annotation
- Handwritten page number at bottom right

## Forbidden
- No real paper texture photos (too messy)
- No narrative comics or character dialogue boxes
- No old diary, parchment, treasure map, or yellowed archives
- No childish cartoon style
